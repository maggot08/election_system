About Mister Nepal


The Mr. Nepal competition is a male beauty pageant organized by Expose Nepal. It was founded in 2002. The entrants compete in various activities including swimming, Extreme challenge and physique, Talent performances, mountain climbing, and marathon running. The current Mr. Nepal is Dr. Santosh Upadhyaya of Achham who was crowned on December 26, 2019 in Kathmandu, Nepal. Traditionally, Mr Nepal lives in Kathmandu during his reign.
